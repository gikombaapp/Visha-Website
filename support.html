Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from our community.

How do I reset my password? expand_more
You can reset your password by clicking on 'Forgot Password' at the login screen. We will send an email with instructions to your registered address within a few minutes.
Can I change my subscription plan at any time? expand_more
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your Account Settings. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately.
What is your refund policy? expand_more
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our premium plans. If you're not satisfied, just contact our support team and we'll process your refund, no questions asked.
Is my data secure? expand_more
Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and follow best practices to ensure your data is always protected and never shared with third parties.

Still need help?

Our friendly team is here to help. Send us a message and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
