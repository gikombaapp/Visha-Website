Fast fashion is one of the largest polluters globally. Thrifting extends the life of clothing and reduces waste.

## The Impact

- **Less water waste** – Producing one cotton shirt takes 2,700 liters of water
- **Reduced landfill** – 85% of textiles end up in landfills
- **Lower carbon footprint** – No new manufacturing required

Thrifting isn't just affordable — it's responsible.
