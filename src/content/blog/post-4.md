From CBD to Kilimani, Nairobi's fashion scene is thriving. We spoke with local style icons about their thrift finds and fashion philosophy.

## Featured Styles

- **Layered vintage** – Mixing thrifted blazers with modern fits
- **Bold patterns** – African prints paired with denim
- **Minimalist thrift** – Clean, neutral-toned secondhand pieces

Nairobi's street style proves that great fashion doesn't require a big budget.
