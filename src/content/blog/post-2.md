Vintage denim has exploded in popularity across Nairobi's thrift markets. From Gikomba to Toi Market, shoppers are hunting for 90s Levi's, distressed fits, and unique washes.

## Where to Shop

- **Gikomba Market** – The largest thrift market in East Africa
- **Toi Market** – Great for curated vintage finds
- **Online thrift stores** – Instagram shops specializing in vintage denim

The trend shows no signs of slowing down.
